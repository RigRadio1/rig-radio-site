Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Rig-Radio (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and services.

1) What We Collect

  • Account info — name, email, password (hashed), and settings you provide.
  • Content you upload — tracks, artwork, and related metadata (title, artist, file paths, timestamps, status).
  • Usage data — pages viewed, actions (e.g., plays/likes), device/browser basics, and IP address for security/anti-abuse.
  • Cookies & local storage — to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and store your DJ queue locally on your device.
  • Support messages — info you send via the contact page or email.

2) How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve Rig-Radio (streaming, library, DJ booth, playlists).
  • Authenticate users, secure the service, and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • Communicate with you about your account, policy changes, or service updates.
  • Analyze aggregate usage to improve features and performance.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and comply with law.

3) Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to keep sessions and preferences working. Blocking cookies may break login or playback. We do not sell cookie data.

4) Analytics & Advertising

We currently do not run third-party ad networks. If we add privacy-respecting analytics in the future, we’ll update this page and limit data to what’s necessary.

5) Sharing Information

  • Service providers — e.g., hosting and storage (Supabase, VPS). They only process data to help us run Rig-Radio and must protect it.
  • Legal — we may disclose information to comply with law, a valid legal request, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.
  • Business changes — if we transfer or merge the service, information may move with it under the same protections.

6) Data Retention

We keep account and upload data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. We may retain limited records after deletion to meet legal, security, or backup requirements.

7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption at rest by providers, hashed passwords, access controls). No system is 100% secure; use strong passwords and protect your login.

8) Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/Update — you can view or update profile details from your account (where available) or by contacting us.
  • Delete — request deletion of your account/content; we’ll remove what we can, except where retention is required by law or security.
  • Preferences — browser settings let you manage cookies and local storage.

Some rights vary by region. We honor applicable law where you reside; requests may require identity verification.

9) Children’s Privacy

Rig-Radio is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

10) International Users

We operate in the United States. If you access Rig-Radio from outside the U.S., you understand your data may be processed and stored in the U.S. under U.S. law.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the new version with a revised “Last updated” date. Your continued use of Rig-Radio after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
